About Hon. Muhwezi Yonani

Hon. Muhwezi Yonani is the District Councillor for Kamwenge Sub-county and Minister of Works and Production for Kamwenge District. He works closely with district leadership, LC5, religious leaders, and communities to promote development and unity.

He is a committed, hardworking, friendly, and collaborative leader serving under the National Resistance Movement (NRM).
